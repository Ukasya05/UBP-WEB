Our Real Estate Development Services

At PT. Ukasaya Bangun Persada, we specialize in comprehensive real estate development projects that include residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Our expertise ensures quality, sustainability, and value in every development.

  • Residential Projects: Designing and building modern housing, apartments, and gated communities tailored to diverse needs.
  • Commercial Spaces: Development of office buildings, retail centers, and mixed-use complexes with prime locations.
  • Industrial Areas: Planning and constructing industrial parks and warehouses with efficient logistics and infrastructure.
  • Project Management: End-to-end management from land acquisition to construction and handover.
  • Sustainability: Incorporating green building practices and energy-efficient designs.
